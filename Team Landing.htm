pay

Werner Enterprises’ dedication to offering drivers the best lifestyle means giving our drivers high pay combined with high production. Teams average $100k-$150k per year plus Werner offers a monthly mileage pay incentive as well.

Werner Enterprises’ production is 10 percent higher than our competitors, making Werner Enterprises’ drivers some of the highest paid in North America. Werner offers professional drivers many additional benefits, including affordable life, disability, health, dental and vision insurance, and 401(k) retirement and stock purchase plans. Call 888.209.5171 to start your career as a team driver today.

equipment

As a team driver, you will drive the newest equipment, and after six months you will qualify to have a new inverter installed. Werner provides the best equipment in the industry, including Freightliners, Peterbilts and Kenworths. With an average truck age of 1.7 years or less, our late-model equipment has added safety and technology features, which allow you to efficiently and comfortably do your job. Werner’s safety and technology features include automatic transmissions, collision avoidance systems and lane departure safety systems to help you share the roads safely. Werner Enterprises supplies you with all the necessities and luxuries you deserve.

team matching

If you don’t have a partner, but are interested in running full team, we will help match you with a teammate through the Werner team matching program. Werner’s team matching program uses your personal interests to pair you with a driver with similar interests. Call a team matching specialist at 800.848.2187 and begin your new career as a team driver today.

home time

Our strong freight base provides you with the miles you need to stay on the road while still offering you the flexibility of having quality time at home. If you have ever considered becoming a team driver, there is no better time to take that next step.

sign-on bonus

Could you use some extra money? Werner has excellent team driver opportunities waiting for you with several incentives that pay well. As a team driver, you qualify for a $2,500 sign-on bonus, paid out over seven to nine months.

average 5,000 miles per week

As a team driver, you will be assigned the newest equipment in the fleet and will be dispatched on the longest length of haul and timely pre-assigned load information. When you drive as a team, you enjoy the advantages of priority load planning as well as a higher percentage of no touch freight.
